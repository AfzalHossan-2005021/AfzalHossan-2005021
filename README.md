## About

I enjoy turning computer science fundamentals into working software: from compiler construction and operating-system concepts to full-stack applications, simulation, and machine-learning experiments. My current work leans toward applied ML, spatial transcriptomics, data-driven modeling, and well-structured engineering projects.

- Based in Dhaka, Bangladesh
- Studying at BUET
- Interested in software architecture, algorithms, systems programming, AI/ML, and computational biology
- Comfortable moving between research prototypes, coursework implementations, and production-minded web/backend systems
